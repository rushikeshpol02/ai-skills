# /transcribe-meeting — Transcript to Meeting Notes

Turns a raw meeting transcript (.vtt, .md, .txt, .docx) into a structured summary with decisions, action items, and open questions.

## Invocation

```
/transcribe-meeting [attach transcript file]
/transcribe-meeting [paste transcript content]
/transcribe-meeting Discovery call transcript — [attach .vtt file]
```

## What you'll get

A structured meeting summary covering:
- Meeting context and attendees
- Key decisions made
- Action items with owners
- Open questions and follow-ups
- Any requirements signals or feature insights

## Workflow

Apply the **transcript-to-meeting-notes** skill with the transcript as input.
